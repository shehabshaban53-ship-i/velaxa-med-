Hair pathway

Hair transplant starts with donor quality and target density.

  • Multi-angle photo review

    Front, top, both sides, crown, and donor area images help define technique suitability and the likely graft strategy.

  • Technique discussion

    DHI, FUE, and Sapphire FUE are compared against the case itself, not against trends or generic package language.

  • Recovery expectations

    Patients need clear guidance on washing, redness, swelling, shedding, and the first follow-up steps before they travel.

Dental pathway

Dental care starts with scans, function, and restoration design.

  • Photos and imaging

    Panoramic X-rays, intraoral photos, and consultation notes make implant and restorative planning more precise.

  • Treatment sequencing

    Implant, crown, and smile design cases often require staging decisions based on diagnostics and healing.

  • Material and shade choice

    Crowns and smile makeover cases need a clear balance between strength, translucency, fit, and the final aesthetic target.
