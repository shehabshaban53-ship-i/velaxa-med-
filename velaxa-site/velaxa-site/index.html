Treatment process

A simple path from the first message to treatment follow-up.

  1. 01

    Case intake

    The patient shares the treatment interest, preferred language, available photos, and any existing X-rays or scan reports.

  2. 02

    Medical evaluation

    The case is reviewed to understand suitability, technique options, clinical priorities, and expected scope of care.

  3. 03

    Personalized treatment plan

    The patient receives a clear outline of the likely treatment path, treatment scope, timing, and next booking step.

  4. 04

    Treatment in Istanbul

    Clinical appointments, diagnostics, and treatment-day timing are aligned once the case is confirmed.

  5. 05

    Follow-up

    Post-treatment instructions, recovery checks, and future updates remain on one channel with the team.

FAQ

Questions patients usually ask before they confirm the next step.

How do I start a consultation?

Send hair photos, smile photos, or a panoramic X-ray on WhatsApp. A short written summary is enough to start the case review.

Do I need an X-ray for dental care?

A panoramic X-ray is helpful for implant and wider restorative cases. If you do not have one yet, the team can advise on the next diagnostic step.

How is the treatment plan decided?

The treatment plan is shaped by clinical findings such as donor strength, bone support, gum condition, bite, smile goals, and the scale of work required.

Do you help once the treatment dates are confirmed?

Yes. Once dates are fixed, the treatment schedule and practical visit timing are aligned so the patient knows the next step clearly.
