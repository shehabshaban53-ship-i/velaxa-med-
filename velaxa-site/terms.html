Website terms

The Velaxa website provides general treatment information and consultation routes. Final treatment direction always depends on direct case review and clinical assessment.

  • Informational purpose

    Website content is informational and should not be treated as a substitute for direct medical or dental assessment.

  • Consultation requests

    Messages, forms, and shared files help start the consultation process, but treatment suitability is confirmed only after case review.

  • Communication channels

    WhatsApp and email are used to continue consultation communication, request files, and share next-step instructions.
