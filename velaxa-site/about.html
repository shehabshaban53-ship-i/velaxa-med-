About Velaxa

Premium hair transplant and dental care presented through a cleaner clinic-style experience in Istanbul.

Velaxa focuses on direct consultation, structured medical evaluation, and treatment planning across hair transplant, dental implants, crowns, and smile makeover care. The public presentation is built around the clinical environment, the treatment rooms, and the doctors guiding the dental cases.

Who we treat

The treatment focus is clear, narrow, and designed for direct consultation.

Hair transplant patients

Patients looking for hairline planning, crown coverage, donor assessment, and a realistic discussion of technique and density.

Implant cases

Patients who need imaging review, implant planning, prosthetic direction, and a structured understanding of staged treatment if needed.

Restorative and cosmetic dentistry

Crown and smile makeover cases that need shape, shade, symmetry, and long-term function discussed together.

Multilingual communication

English, Russian, and Romanian-speaking patients who want a direct and clearer route into consultation.
