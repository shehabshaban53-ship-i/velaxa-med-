Hollywood Smile

A smile makeover depends on symmetry, tooth condition, material choice, and the result the patient wants to live with.

A Hollywood Smile is not one single material or one fixed procedure. It starts with smile analysis, tooth condition, gum display, and the balance between brightness, shape, and believable facial harmony.

Smile makeover logic

The right makeover route depends on how much change the smile actually needs.

Smile analysis

The plan starts with tooth size, gum display, lip line, facial proportions, and the overall shape the patient wants to reach.

Tooth condition review

Existing fillings, wear, fractures, and old crowns matter because they affect whether a conservative plan is possible.

Shade and finish

Brightness should be selected carefully so the final look stays clean and attractive without looking artificial.

Veneers vs crowns

Some smile cases suit a lighter veneer approach. Others need crowns for stability and coverage.

  • Veneer-led planning

    Works better when tooth structure is relatively healthy and the goal is mostly aesthetic refinement.

  • Crown-led planning

    More appropriate when teeth already carry restorations, structural loss, or functional demands that need broader coverage.

  • Case-by-case selection

    The final plan should follow tooth condition and long-term function, not only the look of the target smile.

  • Preview conversation

    Patients should understand shape direction, shade preference, and overall smile character before treatment begins.
